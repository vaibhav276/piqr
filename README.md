piqr
====

Chrome Extension to quickly get a QR code for any selected text

You can use this extension to quickly generate QR code for any text on your browser and scan the QR code from your phone/table to get that text on your phone/browser.

1. Simply select text on the web page (it could be any link or plain text)
2. Right click to get a QR code on top right of your browser
3. Scan the QR code from your phone/tablet to get the link/text on your phone

Simple!!
